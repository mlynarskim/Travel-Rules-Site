Van Kitchen: How to Cook Delicious and Healthy Meals on Two Burners?

Tastes Straight from the Road

Cooking in a camper is the art of minimalism. With limited space and usually just two burners, you must rely on smart solutions. Here are a few rules to keep your meals nutritious and cheap:

  • One pot meals: These are essential for travel. They save washing water and gas. Risotto, curry, or thick soups are perfect choices for dinner under the stars.
  • Local ingredients: Instead of stocking up at large supermarkets, shop at local markets. It is the best way to experience the local culture and eat the freshest products.
  • Herbs and spices: They take up little space but can turn simple pasta into a great dish. Keep your set in airtight containers.
  • Cabinet organization: Use baskets and dividers so that glass jars do not make noise while driving.

Eating out is one of the biggest travel expenses. To save money, plan your shopping and meals ahead of time. Use Travel Planer App to monitor your food costs in the expenses section. After cooking a delicious meal, find the perfect picnic spot. Use Travel Rules to locate nearby parks or viewpoints with benches where your meal will taste even better.
