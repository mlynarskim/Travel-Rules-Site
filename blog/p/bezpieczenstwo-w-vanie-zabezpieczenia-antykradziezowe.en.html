Van Security: How to Effectively Protect Your Home on Wheels?

Feel Safe in Your Home on Wheels

Fear of break-ins is a common concern. While Europe is generally safe, it's wise to use caution and secure your vehicle properly.

1. Mechanical Security

  • Door Locks: Additional deadbolts for cab and rear doors.
  • Steering Wheel Lock: A classic visual deterrent.

2. Electronics

  • GPS Tracker: Real-time location monitoring via your smartphone.
  • Gas Alarm: Detects narcotic gases and carbon monoxide.

3. Good Habits

  • Hide Valuables: Keep laptops and wallets out of sight.
  • Plan Secure Stops: Use Travel Planer to budget for secure parking. When looking for safe daytime spots, consult Travel Rules to find well-maintained parks and recreation areas.

4. Emergency Prep

Keep cloud copies of your documents. You can also export your travel logs from Travel Planer as a PDF for insurance purposes.
