Van Life Workation: How to Set Up an Office on Four Wheels

Your New Office with a View

Remote work from a van is no longer just for programmers. It's a lifestyle that blends professional duties with exploring new places. To ensure your workation is a success, focus on these essentials:

  • Reliable Internet: An LTE/5G router with an external antenna is standard. Having two SIM cards from different providers helps maintain connectivity across borders.
  • Power Solutions: Solar panels and LiFePO4 batteries are mandatory for off-grid work. An inverter will allow you to charge your laptop just like at home.
  • Ergonomics: Your van table must be at the right height. If you spend 8 hours at a monitor, invest in a swivel passenger seat—it's usually the most comfortable spot.
  • Work-Life Balance: Change your scenery after work. iPhone users can check Travel Rules to find nearby parks or recreation areas to stretch their legs after a day in front of the screen.
