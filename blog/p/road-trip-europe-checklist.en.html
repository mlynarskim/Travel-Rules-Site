Road trip Europe checklist: what you need before crossing borders

Vehicle documents

Before entering Europe, make sure you have: driver's license, vehicle registration, green insurance card. That's the minimum.

Vignettes — you'll need them

Many European countries require vignettes (paper or electronic stickers) to drive on motorways. Austria, Czech Republic, Hungary, Slovakia, Slovenia — almost everyone requires them.

You always buy a vignette BEFORE entering a country. In Austria: 10 days for 9 euros. In Switzerland: 40 francs per year.

Mandatory equipment

Warning triangle, reflective vest (required in France for each passenger), spare bulbs, jumper cables.
