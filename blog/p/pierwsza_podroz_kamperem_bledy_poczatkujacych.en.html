First Camper Trip: 5 Mistakes That Can Ruin Your Journey

Your First Trip: Preparation is Key

Many people hit the road with a vision of total freedom, forgetting the technical aspects of life on the move. While spontaneity is important, certain mistakes can ruin your holiday. Here are the most common ones:

  • Overloading the vehicle: Beginners often take too many items, affecting fuel consumption and safety. Every kilogram matters for the gross vehicle weight.
  • Overly ambitious routes: Planning 800 kilometers a day leads straight to exhaustion. Van life is about slow discovery, not a race.
  • Ignoring service points: Lack of knowledge on when to empty grey water or how to refill clean water is a common issue.
  • No system test: It is wise to check the heating, water pump, and fridge before leaving home.
