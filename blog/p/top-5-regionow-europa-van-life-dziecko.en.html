TOP 5 European Regions for Your First Van Life with Kids

Where to start?

Your first family van life journey needs a 'soft landing'. We’ve selected places where logistics are simple and child-friendly attractions are easily accessible:

  • Slovenia: Europe in a nutshell. It’s small, green, and incredibly clean. Distances between attractions rarely exceed an hour, perfect for a smooth start.
  • Brittany, France: A camper’s paradise. The network of motorhome stops (Aire) is flawless, and the flat coastal areas are ideal for cycling with a trailer.
  • Denmark: Hygge Standard. A country designed entirely with families in mind. The playgrounds here are works of art, and campsites offer the highest standards in Europe.
  • South Tyrol, Italy: Effortless mountains. Most cable cars are stroller-friendly, and there are interactive educational trails for toddlers at the summits.
  • Algarve, Portugal: The best choice for off-season travel. Wide sandy beaches, a mild climate, and incredibly friendly locals make it a perfect destination for slow travel.
