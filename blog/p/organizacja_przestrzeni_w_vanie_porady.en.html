The Art of Organization: How to Keep Order in a Small Expedition Car?

Order is the Key to a Peaceful Journey

Living in a car teaches minimalism quickly. Every item must have its permanent place. If you allow chaos, you will soon feel tired of the cramped space. Here is how to manage your equipment wisely:

  • Transparent boxes rule: Storing small items in sealed, clear containers allows you to see the contents immediately.
  • Use vertical space: Van walls are perfect for storage nets and hooks. Keep essentials like keys and flashlights there.
  • Clothing selection: Take only items that match each other and can be layered. Avoid things you will not wear at least three times a week.
  • Regular cleaning: In a small car, dirt is visible instantly. Spending five minutes on tidying makes a huge difference.

When packing, use Travel Planner App to manage your preparations. If the van feels too small, step outside. Travel Rules will help you find the nearest recreation area to recharge in nature.
