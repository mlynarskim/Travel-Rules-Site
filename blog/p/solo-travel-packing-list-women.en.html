Solo travel packing list for women: what I never forget before flying abroad

Safety in solo travel

Traveling solo as a woman requires extra preparation. Safety is not paranoia — it's being ready.

Your backpack must include: personal alarm (130dB siren), door stopper, small flashlight, drink covers, USB-C charger (your phone is your lifeline).

Documents and copies

Before you fly, make digital copies of all documents: passport (front and back), driver's license, insurance, tickets. Email yourself a copy. Install Travel Rules — the app stores all documents offline.

Physical copies: printed or photos in a notebook.

Toiletries and hygiene

100ml bottles (TSA limit), not full packages. Tampons, pads and intimate cream — bring from home, as they may be unavailable or expensive abroad.

Antibiotic (prescription from your doctor — for UTIs), probiotic, anti-diarrheal pills.

Technology

20000mAh power bank, USB-C cable, adapter for power outlets in your destination, wireless headphones (for safety).
