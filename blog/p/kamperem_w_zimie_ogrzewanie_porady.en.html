Winter in a Camper: How to Stay Warm and Safe on Frigid Days?

Stay Warm Inside Despite the Cold Outside

Winter travels are becoming more popular. The key to success is an efficient heating system. Most common are diesel heaters like Webasto or gas systems like Truma. It is important to ventilate the interior regularly to prevent moisture condensation on walls and windows.

Water tank insulation is another must. If your car lacks a heated water garage, use special heating mats. Monitoring fuel and gas consumption in winter is crucial for safety. Using Travel Planner App, you can track these expenses. During winter walks, Travel Rules will help you find cleared parks and recreation areas for a short rest.
