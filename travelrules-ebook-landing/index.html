What goes wrong on a first camper trip?

Most first-timers don’t fail because they lack gear — they fail because they miss the small essentials.

  • Forgetting critical items (adapters, hoses, leveling blocks, etc.)
  • Rushing safety checks and missing something obvious
  • Overloading the camper or packing weight in the wrong places
  • Arriving late with no simple “arrival routine”
  • Getting stuck with a basic issue: water / power / gas
  • Underestimating real costs (fuel, tolls, campsites, hidden fees)

This ebook gives you a simple system to prevent these problems.
